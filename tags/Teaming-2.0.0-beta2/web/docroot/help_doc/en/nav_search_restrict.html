Restricting Searches to One Place

To restrict searches to one workspace or folder, do the following:

  1. Click Advanced.

  2. In the Advanced search form, click the plus sign (+) next to the top workspace (by default, labeled Workspaces), move through the hierarchy, and select the box next to the workspace or folder you wish to search.

    Use the plus sign and minus sign icons, and do not click the titles of workspaces and folders.

  3. If you want Kablink Teaming to search every place below your selection in the workspace tree, then select Search all sub places.

  4. Specify any additional advanced search criteria.

  5. Click Search, in the lower-right corner of the top portion of the form.
