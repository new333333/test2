My Workspace

Click the My workspace icon to view your SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise home page. This fun and attractive personal workspace can contain:

  • Your picture and other favorite photos

  • Contact information

  • Your blog

  • Your personal calendar

  • A guestbook that others can sign

  • And much more!

If you are a new user of SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise, then getting comfortable with your personal workspace is a great way to start. For example, you can write an introduction to your workspace, upload a few pictures, and add a blog entry (journal entry). As visitors to your personal workspace, other people are allowed to see your contact information, view your blog entries, and comment on them.
