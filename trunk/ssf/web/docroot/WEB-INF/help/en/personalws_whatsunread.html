What’s Unread

List new the entries in the current folder or workspace and in any folders beneath this folder or workspace that you have not visited. Entries are considered to be new if they have been created or modified within the past 30 days. Entries older than 30 days are treated as if they were seen and are dropped from the list. If someone adds a comment to an entry, this marks the entry as new.
