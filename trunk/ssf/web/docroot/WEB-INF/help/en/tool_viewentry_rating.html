Rating

Knowledge management and social networking applications provide a way for people to determine which entries are most popular. SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise provides a five-star rating tool for this purpose.

When viewing an entry, SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise displays yellow stars to indicate the average rating for this entry, with one star being the least favorable rating and five stars being the most favorable rating. If all stars are gray, then no one rated the entry yet.

To submit your rating, click a star (for example, to submit a three-star rating, click the third star from the left). You can change your rating as often as you want, but SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise uses only one rating (your latest rating) when calculating the average.
