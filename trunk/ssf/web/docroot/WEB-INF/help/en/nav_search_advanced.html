Using Advanced Search

Use advanced search if you want to limit your search based on various additional criteria, including the author of the item, one specific workspace or folder, a category of item (only folders or only personal workspaces), a workflow state, an applied tag, an activity date, and more.

You can access the advanced search form from either the navigation bar or from a search results page; after a quick search, you can click Advanced to switch to an advanced search. If you want to include more than the initially provided criteria, click the More options link in the upper-right corner. (Click the Hide options link to hide the additional criteria again.)

If your search requires more than one item of criteria, click the More criteria... button, and SiteScape ICEcore Enterprise provides another drop-down list for your use. Click the X icon next to a criterion drop-down list to remove it from the form.
