Rating Entries

Like many knowledge management and social networking applications, Kablink Teaming provides a way for people to determine which entries are most popular. Teaming supplies a five-star rating tool for this purpose.

Viewing Entry Ratings

When viewing an entry, Teaming displays yellow stars to indicate the average rating for this entry, with one star being the least favorable rating and five stars being the most favorable rating. If all stars are gray, the entry has not been rated.

Submitting Entry Ratings

To submit your rating, click a star. (For example, to submit a three-star rating, click the third star from the left.) You can change your rating as often as you want, but Teaming uses only your most-recent rating when calculating the average.
