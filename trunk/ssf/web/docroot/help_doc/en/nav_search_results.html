Using the Search Results Page

After conducting a search, the search results page is displayed. The search results page provides tools in the left sidebar, displays search criteria at the top of the right column, and lists search results in the main section of the page, as described in the following sections:

Tools

The left sidebar provides the following tools to help make your search more efficient:

Recent Places: Notice that, as you conduct searches, Kablink Teaming places links to each search in the Recent Places section of the tools sidebar, so you can easily perform a recent search again. However, because Kablink Teaming saves only a few of your most recently visited places and searches, use Saved searches for searches that you want to make permanently available to you, until you decide to delete the search.

Saved searches: Provide a name for your current search and click Save. Later, in the navigation bar, you can click the drop-down arrow next to the Search icon and click the linked name of a saved search you wish to conduct again.

Top Ranked Places: With items at the top of the list being most relevant, this list tells you the places where your search topic is most often being discussed.

Top Ranked People: With items at the top of the list being most relevant, this list tells you the people who are most often contributing to the discussions about the topic you searched.

Tags: Shows tags that have been applied to matched items.

Search Criteria

If your initial search did not yield the desired results, the Quick search section enables you to perform another search. Enter your desired topic to search for in the Text field, then click Search.

If you want to perform a more in-depth search, click Advanced.

Search Results

In the main section of the right column, Kablink Teaming displays the results of your search. To view one of the results, click on the name of the result. Results can be files, entries, or anything else in Kablink Teaming.
