Workspace Tree Navigation

The Workspace tree displays the path of all of the workspaces and folders that contain the place you are currently viewing.

This tree also enables you to navigate to any other place you want to go within Kablink Teaming, but it does so without leaving the current page. When you click a workspace or folder link in the tree, Teaming displays the new place. To return to the page you were previously using, click its linked title in the Recent places section of the main sidebar.

Click a plus sign (+) to see an item's sub-workspaces or sub-folders. Click a minus sign (-) to collapse it.

Click the linked title of a place, and Teaming navigates to that page.

To get started, click the plus sign next to your organization's top-level workspace. Teaming displays links to the global, personal, and team workspaces. These are default workspaces that are containers for specific categories of work.
