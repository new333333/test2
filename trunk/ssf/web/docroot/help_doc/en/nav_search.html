Search

Type words or quoted phrases into the Search field, and either press Enter on your keyboard or click the Search icon.

Novell TeamingKablink presents a full-paged, maximized view, and displays a list of links to any item anywhere in Novell TeamingKablink that contains all of the words or phrases you specified. When you are finished with this search, if you want to return to the previous page, click the left arrow icon in the upper-right corner of the page, or use other navigation tools to go to the page of your choice.

Clicking Advanced performs a more refined search. The drop-down arrow next to the Search icon displays links to saved searches.

In the Find section, the People, Places, and Tags fields provide a way to quickly locate specific types of items. When using these fields, Novell TeamingKablink applies the "type to find" feature. As you type, Novell TeamingKablink lists items matching what you have typed so far (all items matching "d", then all items matching "di", for example). If there is more than one panel of choices, you can click the left arrow icons and right arrow icons to review your choices.
