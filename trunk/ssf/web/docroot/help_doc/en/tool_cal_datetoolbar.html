Date Toolbar

The date tool bar in Kablink Teaming enables you to customize the Calendar folder view.

This tool bar allows you to specify:

  • Whether Teaming should display all hours in the day, or only regular working hours.

  • The amount of time displayed on a single folder page (1 day, 3 days, 5 days, 1 full week, two weeks, or a month).

  • The next or previous folder page, using the Left and Right arrow icons. For example, if you are currently viewing three days of information, clicking the arrows shows you the previous or next three days of information.

  • When you click the Calendar Settings icon, you can specify the following options:

    • On what day of the week the calendar starts, whether it be a Saturday, Sunday, or Monday.

    • At what time your work day starts.

  • The month or day to display, using the calendar applet. After selecting a new month to view, be sure to click OK to refresh the folder page.
