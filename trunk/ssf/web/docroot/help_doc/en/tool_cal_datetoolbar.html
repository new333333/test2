Date Toolbar

The date tool bar alters how you view a folder that organizes its information according to one or more dates (for example, calendar and task folders). Depending on the type of folder you are using, you might not see every tool.

This tool bar allows you to specify:

  • That Kablink Teaming should count all hours in the day, or only regular working hours.

  • The amount of time displayed on a single folder page (1 day, 3 days, 5 days, 1 full week, two weeks, or a month).

  • The next or previous folder page, using the Left arrow icon and Right arrow icon. For example, if you are currently viewing three days of information, clicking the arrows show you the previous or next three days of information.

  • On what day of the week the calendar starts, whether it be a Saturday, Sunday, or Monday. Also, at what time your work day starts

  • The month or day to display, using the calendar applet. This applet looks like a page from a hard copy calendar, and you can click on the name of the month or the number of the day you want to view. After selecting a new month to view, be sure to click OK to refresh the folder page.
