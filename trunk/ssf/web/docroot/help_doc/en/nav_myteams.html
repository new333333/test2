My Teams

Clicking the My teams icon displays links to all global and team workspaces in which you are a team member. Click a linked title to visit the workspace or folder. When you are finished, click the Close icon (X) in the upper right corner of the list to close it.

Membership in a team can determine who can view or participate in a workspace or folder. Also, membership in a team can quicken the speed of configuration and communication. For example, while working in a team area, you can add all members of a team quickly to a buddy list or to the clipboard.
