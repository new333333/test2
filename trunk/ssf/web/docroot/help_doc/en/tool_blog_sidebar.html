Blog Sidebar

The Blog sidebar in Kablink Teaming provides the following tools for locating and viewing blog entries:

Blog Pages

If you have multiple pages in your blog folder, the pages are listed at the top of the Blog sidebar. To navigate to a different page in the folder, click the linked name of the page.

Archives

The Blog sidebar provides an Archives section that lists all the months when entries were made to the blog folder. When you click a month, all of the blog entries created in that month are displayed in reverse chronological order, with the newest entries displayed at the top of the list.
