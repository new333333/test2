Using Advanced Search

Use advanced search if you want to limit your search based on various additional criteria, including the author of the item, one specific workspace or folder, a type of item (only folders or only personal workspaces), a workflow state, an applied tag, an activity date, and more.

You can access the advanced search form either the navigation bar or from a search results page; after a quick search, you can click Advanced to switch to an advanced search. If you want to include more than the initially provided criteria, click Revise your Advanced Search query in the Summary of search criteria section.

If your search requires more than one item of criteria, click Add and Kablink Teaming provides another field for your use. Click the X icon next to a criterion to remove it from the search.

To save this search for later use, provide a name in the Saved searches section of the tools side bar on the left side of the page, and click Save.
