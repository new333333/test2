The Favorites Panel

When you click the Favorites icon, Novell TeamingKablink Teaming displays the Favorites panel. This panel contains links to your favorite workspaces and folders.

To add places to your Favorites panel, use the navigation bar or the workspace tree to view a workspace or folder. After arriving at one of your favorite places, click the Favorites icon, and then click the Add this place link.

The Favorites panel includes tools for organizing links to your favorite places. On the Favorites panel, click the Edit favorites link, select the box next to the desired place, and indicate if you want to move the link up or down in the list, or if you want to delete the link from the Favorites panel.
