Displaying Entries and Contacting People

Several tools in Kablink Teaming (for example, folder listings and search results) display a summary of entry information. The information in this summary enables you to display the entry, or contact the creator of the entry, as described in the following sections.

Displaying Entries

To display an entry, simply click the title of the entry.

You can configure the way Teaming displays entries. When you open an entry, Teaming can display the entry as an overlay, in a new page, or in a pop-up window.

To change the way entries are displayed in Teaming:

  1. In the main sidebar, in the Personal Preferences section, click Entry View Style.

  2. Select your desired method to view the entries.

Contacting People

The entry summary includes a Presence icon, located next to the author's name. This Presence icon has the following status possibilities:

  • Green: The person is online and active.

  • Yellow: The person was active recently, but is now away.

  • Gray: The person is not logged in to the Kablink Conferencing client.

  • White: The person’s status is unknown.

Click the Presence icon and select a menu item to contact the person. You can start an instant meeting or schedule a meeting with the person, call the person, add the person to your clipboard, or view the person’s MiniBlog.
