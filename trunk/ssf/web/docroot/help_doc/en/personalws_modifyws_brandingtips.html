Creating Your Brand

If you want to add a graphic with text, you can insert a two-column, one-row table into the branding editing area. Place your graphic in the left cell and your text in the right cell.

To insert a table:

  1. Click the Insert Table icon.

  2. Specify the properties of the table, then click Insert.

For best results, limit your image size to no more than 100 pixels in height.
