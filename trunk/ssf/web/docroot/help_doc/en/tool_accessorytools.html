Accessory Tools

Accessories are sections that you can add to your Kablink Teaming pages. They provide added functionality, and enhance teamwork and communication. The following are a few examples of accessories:

  • Search results (possibly applied just to the current place)

  • Buddy list (perhaps for local subject matter experts)

  • Workspace tree (opened to this place in the hierarchy)

  • Guestbook

The following topics describe how to use the Accessory Panel:

Adding an Accessory

To add an accessory to the Accessory Panel:

  1. Click Accessory Panel.

  2. In the Accessory Scope section, select the desired accessory scope.

    To learn more about the options in this section, see Configuring Who Can View Accessories.

  3. In the Accessory Type section, select the accessory type that you want to add.

  4. Click OK.

  5. Provide a title for the accessory, and fill in any other relevant information.

  6. Click Apply > Close.

Configuring Who Can View Accessories

When adding an accessory, you can choose the accessory scope. In the Accessory Scope section, you have the following options:

  • Personal Accessory for This Page Only: The accessory applies to only the current place, and only you can view the accessory.

  • Personal Accessory for all Workspaces and Folders: The accessory applies to all workspaces and folders, and only you can view the accessory.

  • Community Accessory for This Page: The accessory applies to this page only, and can be viewed by all Teaming users who have access rights to the page.

Changing the Order of Accessories

Teaming enables you to rearrange accessories on any given page where more than one accessory exists.

  1. In the upper right corner of the accessory’s title bar, click the up-arrow or down-arrow, depending on whether you want to move the accessory up or down.

Re-Configuring an Accessory

Teaming enables you to reconfigure an accessory after it has already been created.

  1. In the upper right corner of the accessory’s title bar, click the Modify icon.

Deleting an Accessory

  1. In the upper-right corner of the accessory’s title bar, click the Delete icon.

Hiding Accessories

Teaming enables you to hide accessories, so only the accessory’s title bar is visible.

  1. In the upper right corner of the accessory’s title bar, click the Hide icon.

You can also simultaneously hide all accessories in the Accessory Panel.

  1. Click the Hide icon next to Accessory Panel.

    All of the accessories on the current page are hidden from view.

    Click the Hide icon again to reveal the accessories.
