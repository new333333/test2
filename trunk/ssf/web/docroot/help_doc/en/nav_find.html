Finding Specific People, Places, or Tagged Items

The Find feature is a quick way to navigate to a certain place within Kablink Teaming. Use the Find feature to locate people, places, and tags that you know exist on the Teaming site. If you are looking for a subject-matter expert, or want to see what information is available concerning a particular topic, use Teaming Search functionality.
